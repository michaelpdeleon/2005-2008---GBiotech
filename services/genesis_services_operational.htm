Sherute, L.L.C. (Sherute) is a multi-disciplinary construction management and facilities service company providing professional, efficient and cost-effective solutions to small and large commercial clients in New Jersey.

Headquartered within one mile of GBC, Sherute is ideally located to service not only the needs of the Campus, but also the needs of the scientists within the GBC community.

Sherute provides campus-wide services such as Landscaping and Snow Removal, Construction Management and Security Services as well as common area maintenance and custodial services. For the individual community members, Sherute’s maintenance and custodial staff of experienced and friendly professionals are available to assist you in the maintenance of your work space. Additional services available to the scientific community members include security, mail, shipping, and receiving.

Sherute’s services allow scientists to focus on their actual work and not on the infrastructural details associated with every day operations.

 

  

GBC Community Members can choose from the following Sherute services:

Maintenance

The Sherute Maintenance staff can handle all of your simple repair needs. With one call to Sherute, pictures can be hung, shelves can be mounted, and simple repairs completed. For those more complex maintenance issues, the Sherute staff will arrange for task-specific professional technicians to conduct equipment repairs, instrument calibration and so on. Either way, Sherute has you covered!

Janitorial

Sherute’s full-service Janitorial component is equipped to serve the every day needs of your business. From emptying trash to dusting to vacuuming, mopping, stripping and waxing, the Sherute janitorial staff is available to help you maintain a clean, sanitary working environment.

Construction Management

Sherute’s construction management team can assist you with everything from furniture moves to redesign and construction of your office space.

Security

Sherute’s security services include the installation and maintenance of electronic card access control systems. Sherute can also manage your employee database within its central monitoring system to ensure that your employees have restricted access to specific rooms within your leased space if desired.

Mail/Shipping/Receiving
