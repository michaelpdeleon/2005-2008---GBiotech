  The Genesis Biotechnology Campus (GBC) has been designed to promote the creation and application of new knowledge that will provide economic and social benefits to the community, the environment, and human health through an integrated systems-based scientific strategy. GBC is a place that encourages and supports the creation of innovative projects that cross traditional disciplinary boundaries. For this purpose GBC consolidates research activities in the fields of biotechnology, molecular biology, genome research, biochemistry, nano and biophysics, bioinformatics and computer science on a single campus. This state-of-the-art facility will cultivate research partnerships between highly productive research teams.
